Methods

What tools are at your disposal?

Here are some steps that can help with the practice of situational awareness:

1. Observe

Look around at your setting. What do you see? What is happening? What is going on at that exact moment?

2. Apprehend

Now that you have seen everything around your area, what do you know about it? If you see someone walking, where do you think they are going? Etc.

3. Forecast

The next step, what do you think is going to happen? If you are in a situation where you are unsure of what is going on, then try to predict what will happen.

4. Act

And finally, now that you have an idea of what will happen, now it is your turn. Act accordingly and appropriately to the situation.
