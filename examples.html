Where can you use Situational awareness?

At home:

When at home, situational awareness can be employed whenever there is a chore to be done, and especially when cooking. For example, it is important to know when a meal might be burning by being aware of any smoke in the house.

At the work place:

Situational awareness is insanely important in the work place. Especially when the job is very dynamic and requires a worker to be attentive. If the job were less stressful, it is still important to have situational awareness tools, as you never know when a situation might arise.

At social events:

While situational awareness is important at work and home, it is also important especially in public areas. While in the work place where machinery can be predicable, social events are very unpredictable, every person has different things going on inside their head, so it is important to pay attention to everything and everyone in these situations.
