Situational awareness

Situational awareness has been an issue in the world for quite a while now, and it has been this way especially since the creation of mobile smartphones. Situational awareness by its basic definition is quote: “is the perception of environmental elements and events with respect to time or space.” These elements could be people, hazards, or other dynamic elements that require attention.

What is social awareness?

As described simply above, situational awareness is the perception of the setting around them. For instance, if one were to be crossing a road, it is important to be aware of one's surroundings.

If say this person were to be on their phone, then they have taken their eyes off the setting around them, and now could be subjected to unexpected events.

Situational awareness is also used by the military, as having this skill can greatly boost a soldier's performance in battle, as they are more keen to their surroundings.

Where should you be using social awareness?

Situational awareness is one of those skills that can utilized anywhere, with no drawback or cons in the process. For a start, one of the best and most practiced areas of situational awareness is on the road.

Situational awareness is very important on the road, 9% of car crashes involved a person who was distracted and not paying attention to their surroundings, thanks to Distracted Driving | Transportation Safety | Injury Center | CDC for providing the statistics. What are some of the benefits?

Situational awareness has a number of benefits, one of the biggest ones is that it can increase your safety. Whether it be from driving on the aforementioned roads, or walking through a busy intersection, situational awareness can help with it

Situational awareness is also useful in the army, as many officers will train their soldiers to have hyper-senses when it comes to their surroundings, and that is very important as getting ambushed, or being surprised while in combat is never a great thing.

In conclusion Situational awareness is very important when it comes to one's safety, and utilizing it at its best will result in great results, as you will become more vigilant, and can stay focussed for longer periods of time of time while also staying alert and keeping up with relevant events in your surroundings.
