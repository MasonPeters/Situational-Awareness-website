Situational Awareness

Situational awareness has been an issue in the world for quite a while now, and it has been this way especially since the creation of mobile smartphones. Situational awareness by its basic definition is quote: “is the perception of environmental elements and events with respect to time or space.” These elements could be people, hazards, or other dynamic elements that require attention.
As described above, situational awareness is the perception of the setting around them. For instance, if one were to be crossing a road, it is important to be aware of the surroundings, such as oncoming cars, or other hazards. And this ensures extra safety, hence situational awareness is a great skill to possess.
Situational awareness is one of those skills that can utilized anywhere, with no drawback or cons in the process. One of the best and most practiced areas of situational awareness is on the road. Situational awareness is very important on the road, 9% of car crashes involved a person who was distracted and not paying attention to their surroundings.
Whether it be from driving on the aforementioned roads, or walking through a busy intersection, situational awareness is important. Situational awareness is also useful in the army, as paying attention is quite neccessary in combat. Utilizing situational awareness will result in the ability to keep up with relevant events in your surroundings.
